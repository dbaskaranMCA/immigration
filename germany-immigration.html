What is a job seeker visa?

The Germany Job Seeker Visa is a German National long stay visa that was established in August 2012. The main idea is to lure more qualified experts from abroad to come to Germany to attend interviews and find a job. However, job seekers are not allowed to work in Germany under this type of visa.

The job seeker visa for Germany is a Long-Term Residency Permit , which permits you to remain in the nation for a half year and search for an occupation. In the event that toward the finish of the half year you have discovered a position of business, you will be given the Germany work visa or a Germany work grant and you will be permitted to work and live there. If you don't mind, note that having an occupation searcher visa doesn't permit you to begin working promptly in Germany. It just implies that you can visit the nation and search for work during your stay.
